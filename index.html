About Us

Sticky Pixel is a full service design agency based in Nice on the French Riviera, relentless in the pursuit of creating beautiful, effortless, digital experiences that drive the real-world growth of our clients’ businesses.

Our mission is to tell your story in the best possible way by aligning your online strategy to your business objectives, either short, mid or long term. This could include generating more leads, increasing your brand recognition, converting more prospects to customers or promoting an event etc.

Hence, we don’t just take your order and serve you a website or email campaign. We spend time with you to ask the right questions about who you are, what you’re trying to achieve and how your current marketing activities are performing. Then we put together a digital solution that address those aspirations, combining what you need to say, who you want to say it to and how you will say it.
